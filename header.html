About

St Marys Group Of Institutions

St. Mary's is a reputable institution offering a wide range of high-quality teaching and extra-curricular activities. We are inspired by excellence and innovation, and we provide specialized courses with blended learning opportunities to meet your career needs. Our goal is to create an atmosphere for you to enjoy your learning and upgrade your skills academically and as responsible citizens of our nation. We are affiliated with JNTU Hyderabad, JNTU Kakinada, Osmania University, Mahatma Gandhi University Hyderabad, Acharya Nagarjuna University, and the West Bengal University of Technology. All our courses are approved by AICTE, and our institution is accredited by NBA.

Courses

CSE

The most important aspect of Computer Science and Engineering is problem solving, an essential skill for life. Students study the design, development and analysis of software and hardware used to solve problems in a variety of business, scientific and social contexts. Because computers solve problems to serve people, there is a significant human side to computer science as well.

CSE-AIML

teaching advanced AI and ML innovations. In the 1950s, Minsky and McCarthy defined AI as machine tasks. AI aims to make machines think and act like humans. Alan Turing in 1947 emphasized the need for machines to learn from experience, which is the essence of Machine Learning (ML). ML is a subset of AI, focusing on self-improving algorithms. This program provides a strong foundation in AI and ML, preparing students to contribute to cutting-edge technology.

Diploma(ES)

Embedded systems focuses on teaching students the essential skills and knowledge needed to design, program, and work with specialized computer systems embedded in various devices and applications. This program typically covers hardware components, microcontroller programming, electronics, real-time operating systems, communication protocols, and practical project work, preparing graduates for careers as embedded systems engineers and firmware developers in industries like automotive, consumer electronics, industrial automation, and the Internet of Things (IoT).


Learn more

Diploma

Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) offers a condensed yet comprehensive curriculum. It covers core subjects like programming, data structures, databases, and networking. Students gain knowledge in computer architecture, operating systems, and web development. Cybersecurity fundamentals are often included. Some programs allow specialization in areas like AI or mobile app development. Practical projects and internships provide hands-on experience. Graduates are well-prepared for entry-level roles in software development, IT support, and web development, equipped with valuable technical skills and problem-solving abilities.


Learn more

Data Science

Data Science is indeed a crucial field in our technology-driven world. It encompasses the collection, analysis, and interpretation of vast amounts of data generated by organizations. This data is like raw diamonds, and Data Science serves as the mining infrastructure that extracts valuable insights and patterns from it. ultimately helping businesses make informed decisions and transform their operations.


Learn more

B.Pharmacy

B pharmacy course is well known as a result of various profession choices in pharmaceutical firms, biotechnology firms, clinical segments, private divisions, and open areas. Many medical research companies look for pharmacy graduates providing assorted openings for work which pay them from INR 2 lac to 20 lac . Few significant occupation profiles are Clinical essayist, Medication Safety Associate, Medication Inspector, Pharmaceutical Scientist, etc.


Learn more

Cyber Security

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, and data from malicious threats and attacks. It revolves around three core principles: Confidentiality, ensuring data privacy; Integrity, maintaining data accuracy and trustworthiness; and Availability, making data accessible when needed. Professionals employ various tools like firewalls, encryption, and intrusion detection systems to combat threats, including malware and phishing.
